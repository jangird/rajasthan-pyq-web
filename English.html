CET 25 English Question With Explanation 2025

1. Defunct

Answer: Death
Defunct means no longer working or existing.

2. Fortitude

Answer: Courage
Fortitude means mental strength or courage in difficulties.

3. Morale

Answer: Confidence
Morale refers to confidence or enthusiasm of a person.

4. Pensive

Answer: Thoughtful
Pensive means deeply thoughtful.

5. Amity

Answer: Friendship
Amity means friendly relations.

6. Ceremonious

Answer: Formal
Ceremonious means strictly formal.

7. Luminous

Answer: Bright
Luminous means giving off light.

8. Brisk

Answer: Quick
Brisk means fast or energetic.

9. Gigantic

Answer: Huge
Gigantic means extremely large.

10. Sacrosanct

Answer: Holy
Sacrosanct means sacred or holy.

11. Stationary

Answer: Standing
Stationary means not moving.

12. Jejune

Answer: Poor
Jejune means dull or lacking substance.

13. Bemused

Answer: Confused
Bemused means puzzled or confused.

14. Paucity

Answer: Shortage
Paucity means scarcity or shortage.

15. Vivid

Answer: Clear
Vivid means clear and detailed.

16. Parsimony

Answer: Miser
Parsimony means extreme unwillingness to spend money.

17. Wary

Answer: Cautious
Wary means careful and alert.

18. Embellish

Answer: Decorate
Embellish means to decorate or make attractive.

19. Aversion

Answer: Dislike
Aversion means strong dislike.

20. Swift

Answer: Meteoric
Swift means very fast.

21. Slipshod

Answer: Careless
Slipshod means done without care.

22. Fiasco

Answer: Failure
Fiasco means complete failure.

23. Grisly

Answer: Ghastly
Grisly means frightening or horrible.

24. Humdrum

Answer: Common
Humdrum means dull or ordinary.

25. Citadel

Answer: Fortress
Citadel means a strong fort.
